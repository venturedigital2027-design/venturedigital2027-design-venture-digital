Buying logic

The audit comes before payment because scope matters.

Automation is not a commodity until the workflow is clear. Two businesses can ask for “lead follow-up” and need completely different systems depending on their software, team, lead source, and approval process.

The free audit protects both sides: you get a better recommendation, and we avoid selling a package that does not fit.
